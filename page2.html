Amber Precast originally developed its reputation as a precast concrete and cast stone manufacturer of distinction for residential and commercial projects of all sizes.  

With ever increasing competition in these traditional markets Amber Precast identified niche sectors that would yield profitable work, requiring the business to develop skills that other sector leaders did not have and that differentiated Amber Precast in the precast concrete marketplace. Today Amber Precast provides advanced cementitious technologies and products and in particular provide concrete products for complex, extreme environments where quality and manufacturing skills are far more demanding than the traditional sector norm. 

Whether it’s designing, estimating, mixing or moulding, we provide industry leading high-quality specialist products. As a single-source manufacturer of precast concrete with over 25 years’ experience, Amber Precast sets more than just concrete. We set the standard in our sector.
  1. UNDERCOVER FACILITIES - Dedicated indoor batching plant and mould area to ensure no environmental variations
  2. INDUSTRY LEADING PLANT - State of the art TEKA Mixer guaranteeing mix quality, robust batching plant supporting high density concrete aggregates 
  3. BEST IN CLASS - High tolerance manufacture systems out-classing “normal” sector supply
  4. MEETING HIGHLY REGULATED INDUSTRY DEMANDS - Quality Management Systems that meet Nuclear Standard, engineered process and procedure driven manufacturing plans
  5. INTEGRATED MRP SYSTEM - Manufacture Resource Planning system that meets the high-quality requirements for highly regulated industries such as the nuclear sector 
  6.  SECTOR LEADING RESOURCE SKILLS - Experienced and Multi skilled operators. Sector leading skills in product development / design: experienced seniors who can translate demanding customer requirements in unique solutions and sector leading skills in product delivery: experienced multi skilled operators, who are used to work towards product tolerances, unique in this industry.                    
  7. INTEGRATED WITH ACADEMIA - Close ties with local Universities                                            
  8. ROBUST HIGH QUALITY SUPPLY CHAIN - Linked supply chain for high quality (nuclear & chemical industry compliant) parts and components
